Intro

I'm Guy Shech, a passionate data analyst hailing from the bustling city of Tel Aviv. Currently, I'm immersing myself in the world of Industrial Engineering and Management at Tel Aviv University, approaching the culmination of my academic journey.

Driven by a deep fascination for data analysis, I embark on a continuous quest for insights that shape meaningful decisions. Armed with expertise in Python, SQL, and Power BI, I navigate the intricate landscape of data to uncover hidden patterns and opportunities.

My website serves as a window into my analytical prowess, featuring a collection of projects that epitomize my commitment to data-driven exploration.
